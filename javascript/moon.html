Moon

The moon is Earth's only natural satellite and is the fifth largest moon in our solar system. It's about a quarter of the size of Earth, with a diameter of 2,159 miles.
The moon orbits Earth in a slightly squashed circle called an ellipse, taking 27 days, 7 hours, 43 minutes, and 11.6 seconds to complete one revolution. It travels at a speed of 2,288 mph, and is fastest when it's closest to Earth
