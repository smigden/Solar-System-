Venus
Venus is the second planet in the solar system after Mercury. No trace of life has been discovered on Venus, making it one of the hostile planet. It is the hottest and the deadliest planet with poisonous gases, its atmosphere is composed of carbon dioxide and sulfuric acid.

Venus is the second brightest natural object in the sky. Venus is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty. One day on Venus is longer than one year. Venus has no moons nor rings. Venus is sometimes referred to as the “morning star” and “evening star”. Venus is sometimes called Earth’s sister planet.
